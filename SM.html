Spatial Mapping

Spatial mapping is all about taking information that's tied to specific locations or regions and displaying it visually, often on maps or through graphics. This process involves gathering data linked to geographic points or areas, then examining and interpreting that data to create representations that make it easier to understand and analyze. This visualization can help identify patterns, trends, or relationships between different geographical elements, making complex information more accessible and actionable.
